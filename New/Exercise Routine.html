Beginner Workout Routine

Starting a workout routine can be daunting, but it doesn't have to be. Here is a simple beginner workout routine to get you started:

Day 1: Full Body Workout

- Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., walking, jogging)
- Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
- Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
- Bent-over rows: 3 sets of 12 reps
- Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds
- Cool down: 5-10 minutes of stretching

Day 2: Rest or Light Activity

Take a rest day or engage in light activity like walking or yoga.

Day 3: Lower Body Workout

- Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio
- Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
- Glute bridges: 3 sets of 15 reps
- Calf raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
- Cool down: 5-10 minutes of stretching

Day 4: Rest or Light Activity

Take a rest day or engage in light activity like walking or yoga.

Day 5: Upper Body Workout

- Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio
- Shoulder presses: 3 sets of 12 reps
- Bicep curls: 3 sets of 15 reps
- Tricep dips: 3 sets of 10 reps
- Cool down: 5-10 minutes of stretching

Day 6: Rest or Light Activity

Take a rest day or engage in light activity like walking or yoga.

Day 7: Active Recovery

Engage in an active recovery activity like a light walk, yoga, or stretching.
